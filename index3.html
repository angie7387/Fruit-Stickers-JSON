Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this project?

The purpose of this project is to encourage you, my friend, and all my new friends to find the joy and fun in the everyday. Too, it is to question the politics of such a small, seemingly insignificant object and to delve deeper into such considerations in our everyday.

Where can I find fruit stickers?

Fruit stickers are best harvested at any major grocery store chain.

What are the best practices for harvesting fruit stickers?

1. Generally, the best stickers are around the melons and oranges sections. That's where the designers really pop off.
2. Make sure to check the grocery store for seasonal, fun items with new sticker designs.
3. Bring some parchment paper with you so the stickers can be peeled off easily if needed.
4. Try to find a fruit with a duplicate of stickers on them, as to not make the cashier's job harder if they need to manually type in the PLU code.

How can I support this project?

You can support this project by visiting us regularly for updates and submitting any fruit stickers you've seen. Keep the fruit sticker relevant!
